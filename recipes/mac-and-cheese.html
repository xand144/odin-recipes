Macaroni and Cheese

Credit: Texasfoodgawker / CC BY-SA 4.0

Description

When you're hungry but too lazy to make an effort, give this easy peasy mac n' cheesy recipe a go!

Ingredients

  • 225g macaroni
  • 55g butter
  • 35g flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 500ml milk
  • 300g grated cheddar

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook macaroni in the water, stirring occasionally, until cooked but still firm to the bite (around 8 minutes).
  2. At the same time, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour in milk slowly, stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, while ensuring the milk doesn't burn
  5. Add cheddar and stir until melted.
  6. Drain macaroni and mix into cheese sauce until fully coated.
  7. That's it! Serve hot and enjoy! :)
